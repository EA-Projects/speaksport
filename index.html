How It Works

 How SpeakSport's AI-Receptionist Works for Golf Facilities

SpeakSport is an AI-powered receptionist designed exclusively for golf facilities. When a golfer calls your pro shop, SpeakSport answers instantly - greeting the caller, answering questions, and completing bookings without a staff member picking up the phone.

1 The Call Comes In

When a guest calls your facility, SpeakSport answers with a customized greeting that reflects your brand and tone. The AI is trained on your specific course details — rates, hours, policies, and amenities — so it responds accurately to whatever the caller asks.

2 The AI Handles It

SpeakSport understands golf. Tee times, cart fees, walk-on availability, membership questions - it handles real pro shop conversations naturally and accurately, without putting a single guest on hold, or sending calls to voicemail.

3 You Finally See What's Happening

Every call is logged and transcribed with comprehensive reporting. SpeakSport surfaces insights on call volume, common questions, and guest trends - giving operators a level of visibility into their phone line that simply didn't exist before.
